  Please don't give polar foxes the names of raccoons, red pandas, 
dragons and other foxes with modified models,
otherwise their model will be broken (to fix, use the names of normal foxes)
  You can give your fox any name in any language, but to change its skin,
add next to the name the skin number from the list, for example:
  Charlie (D001); *A012* Harry; Sandy G001
